# SportsMatch - Find Badminton Partners Instantly

A hyperlocal sports matchmaking platform focused on solving the problem of finding reliable badminton partners in Bangalore.

## Problem We're Solving

- **WhatsApp chaos**: 47 messages to organize one game
- **No-shows**: People ghost at the last minute
- **Skill mismatch**: Beginners vs pros, nobody has fun
- **Time waste**: Show up to empty courts

## Our Solution

**"Find badminton partners in 10 minutes, not 10 messages"**

- ⚡ **Lightning fast matching** - Post → Match → Play in 15 minutes
- 🛡️ **Reliability system** - ₹50 deposit ensures 95%+ show-up rate
- 🎯 **Smart matching** - By location, time, and skill level
- 📱 **Mobile-first** - iOS & Android apps

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment**: Vercel
- **Email**: Formspree
